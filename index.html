About Care Basket

Care Basket was created to make the donation process easier, clearer, and more meaningful.
Charity homes provide a list of essential items they currently need, and donors can browse these items directly from the system.
By sponsoring the items listed, donors can ensure their contributions go exactly where they are needed most.

How It Works

Important! User must login first.

Step 1 – Browse Items

View essential items added by the administrator based on charity home needs.

Step 2 – Add to Basket

Select the items you wish to sponsor.

Step 3 – Complete Donation

Proceed to checkout and confirm your sponsorship.
